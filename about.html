About Cody!

Hi there! I'm Cody, a recent college graduate hoping to find the meaning of life in the bottom of a chinese takeout dinner. I grew up in Iowa and stuck around to get my Software Engineering degree at Iowa State. Along the way I spent a semester studying in Denmark and made a return visit at the invitation of LEGO visit their headquarters. In transit I caught the travel bug and haven't stopped since.

I'm currently killing time studying Danish and Spanish in Estonia before I start full-time as a Software Engineer at Microsoft Development Center Copenhagen in Denmark early in 2016. You can follow my adventure at my new blog to stay informed. You can also read about my previous adventures abroad on my old blog. Lastly, check out the video below to hear my take on what it means to be a software engineer.

My wanderlust isn't restricted to "reality" and I often look to books, TV, and film to discover new worlds. BBC greats such as Doctor Who and Sherlock, as well as CW dramas such as Supernatural have stolen my heart. George R. R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire (Game of Thrones) shares the throne with Ender's Game for my favorite fantasy and sci-fi books.
