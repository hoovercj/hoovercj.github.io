About Cody!

Hello everybody. I'm Cody, a recent college graduate hoping to find the meaning of life in the bottom of a chinese takeout dinner. I grew up in Iowa and stuck around to get my Software Engineering degree at Iowa State. Along the way I spent a semester studying in Denmark and made a return visit at the invitation of LEGO visit their headquarters. In transit I caught the travel bug and am itching to keep traveling.

I just finished up my summer as a Software Development Intern for Microsoft in Seattle in the cloud services wing of the Developer Division and am now heading to Germany to begin my Master's studies at RWTH Aachen. I've never had to speak another language to live before, so this is sure to be a wild ride. Follow my adventure at my new blog to stay informed. You can also read about my previous adventures abroad on my old blog

My wanderlust isn't restricted to "reality" and I often look to books, TV, and film to discover new worlds.
